Annual Saga

About

The Annual Saga is a planned multimedia franchise that pays tribute to the 1990s and 2000s. The following are key tenets that will be used to build this franchise:

  • Multimedia Approach: this Annual Saga will consist of video games, comic books, television shows, movies, and a trading card game.
  • Yearly Installments: entries released each year will be tied together with common themes and aesthetics.
  • Cultural Significance: the Annual Saga will provide commentary on technology, trends, psychology, philosophy, and politics.
  • Personal Touch: the Annual Saga will contain autobiographical elements and personal anecdotes to provide a unique flavor to this franchise.
